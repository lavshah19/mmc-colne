WELCOME TO MAHENDRA MULTIPLE CAMPUS

Mahendra Multiple Campus, Nepalgunj is a constituent campus of Tribhuvan University (TU). It was established in 2018 B.S. It’s the oldest campus in this region. Since its establishment, the campus has played the vital role in the production of highly qualified manpower. It is the educational hub in the entire state no. 5 since long.
